🌟 TextLLaMA 🚀

TextLLaMA is your ultimate AI-powered writing assistant, designed to:

  • ✨ Explain complex content in a simple and easy-to-understand way.
  • 📧 Compose and send professional emails efficiently.
  • 🤖 Generate automated replies to emails effortlessly.
  • 🌍 Translate words into multiple languages for global communication.
  • ✅ Improve grammar accuracy for polished and professional content.

🔥 Features

  • 📖 Content Simplification: Break down complex content into simple explanations.
  • ✍️ Email Assistance: Compose and reply to emails effortlessly.
  • 🌐 Translation: Translate text into multiple languages to bridge communication gaps.
  • ✅ Grammar Correction: Detect and fix grammatical errors in your messages and emails.

💻 Powered by Ollama

TextLLaMA leverages the open-source language model by Ollama, delivering exceptional results for grammar correction, word explanations, and translations. A true alternative to tools like Grammarly, designed for efficiency and simplicity.

🚀 Getting Started

  1. 🖥️ Install the Extension: Download and add the extension to your browser.
  2. ✏️ Select the Text: Highlight the text you need to explain, correct, or translate.
  3. 📋 Use Context Menu: Right-click and choose the desired action from the TextLLaMA menu.
